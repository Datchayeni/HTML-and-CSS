When Will Astronauts Sunita Williams And Butch Wilmore Visit White House? Trump Says...

After NASA astronauts Barry 'Butch' Wilmore and Sunita 'Suni' Williams safely returned to Earth after an extended mission aboard the International Space Station, talks began on when they will be invited to the White House. President Donald Trump was asked about it by reporters Wednesday, and he said that the duo is currently recovering after their extended stay abroad at the ISS and will visit the White House as soon as they have fully recovered.
